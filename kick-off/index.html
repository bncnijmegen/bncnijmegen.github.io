BNCNijmegen Kick-Off Meeting

12 September 2025 10:00-12:00 at Huize Heyendael

About #

Are you interested in Biofeedback and Neurofeedback? Do you want to connect with researchers and experts in this exciting field? The Biofeedback & Neurofeedback Community Nijmegen (BNCNijmegen) invites you to our first meeting! Whether you are currently using these methods, considering them for the future, or simply curious about the existing research and expertise on campus, this meeting is for you! Join us to learn more, share ideas, and help shape the future of Biofeedback and Neurofeedback research in Nijmegen.
